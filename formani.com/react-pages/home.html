Obsessed with details

Architecture and interior design are experienced through movement and interaction. At FORMANI, architectural hardware is designed with these moments in mind. What may first appear as a subtle visual element becomes part of the way people move through and experience a space. Over time, FORMANI has grown from a Dutch family business into an internationally recognised designer brand, collaborating with leading architects and interior designers to translate design thinking into hardware. Guided by a deep attention to detail, each design contributes to the overall experience of architecture and interior design.
